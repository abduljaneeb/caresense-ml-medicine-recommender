Ensemble Soft Voting

Three models vote on your symptoms: Random Forest (50%), SVM (35%), and Naive Bayes (15%). The weighted average of their probability outputs decides the final prediction — more robust than any single model.

Severity-Weighted Features

Each symptom is multiplied by its clinical severity weight (1–7) before prediction. Chest pain influences the result far more than mild itching — making predictions clinically smarter.
